How it works

Tell your story. Create journal entries, and write down events as they happen, while your memory is fresh.

Sign and lock your journal entries. We'll store it in a way that meets testimony evidence requirements.

With your entries locked, you won't need to rely on your memory, and you can protect yourself against gaslighting.

If and when you're ready to go to the police, you can simply turn over your entire locker to law enforcement.
